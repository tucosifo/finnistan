The Empire of Finnistan is a micronation founded in Upstate New York by Finn P. Putnam on December 11th, 2021. It borders the Hudson Federation, Dejevesia, Grungolia, and La Hermandad. Finnistan is a constitutional monarchy with elections for President held every 6 months. The government is led by Emperor Finn Putnam and President Jackson Cooper. Finnistan is home to 51 citizens and is the most populous micronation in its local sector. Finnistan claims a total land area of 28 sq mi on its mainland, and over 39,300 sq mi in the autonomous Fuzzbertia province.
