The Empire of Finnistan

The Empire of Finnistan is a micronation founded in Upstate New York by Finn P. Putnam on December 11th, 2021. It borders the Hudson Federation, Dejevesia, Grungolia, and La Hermandad. Finnistan is a constitutional monarchy with elections for President held every 6 months. The government is led by Emperor Finn Putnam and President Jackson Cooper. Finnistan is home to 51 citizens and is the most populous micronation in its local sector. Finnistan claims a total land area of 28 sq mi on its mainland, and over 39,300 sq mi in the autonomous Fuzzbertia province.

Finnistan is home to four provinces and five territories. The largest province, Fuzzbertia, is located in Maine, and got its territory through the expansion of its borders during its time as an independent state from 2018 until unification with Finnistan in 2022, before expanding further in 2023 when they once again became independent from March until December, when they once again unified with Finnistan. The capital, Fyiromá, is home to the Emperor and it is the second largest province. Fyiromá covers an area of 11.2 sq mi in 2 different towns. Louthiros was annexed by Finnistan in February 2023 and is the third largest province and third most populous province. Louthiros notably surrounds the local high school, which is considered an international neutral territory, and is not claimed by any micronation. North Brookview is the fourth largest and fourth most populous province, and was annexed by Finnistan in March 2023, but did not become a province until July.
